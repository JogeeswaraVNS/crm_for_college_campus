About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) started in 1995 is a renowned department for its capacity in R&D aside from its regular academics . B.Tech CSE is NBA Accredited and is recognized as a Research Center by JNTUH. The department offers 2 UG programmes: B.Tech CSE and B.Tech CSBS (Computer Science and Business Systems). It also offers 2 PG programmes: MTech- Software Engineering (SE) and M.Tech-Computer Science and Engineering (CSE).

CSE department has committed and competent faculty who keep abreast with the upcoming technologies, through continuous participation in seminars and workshops, registering for advanced courses, and acquiring merit certifications. Dedicated labs are set up to foster Research in current domains such as Data Sciences, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Gaming. The Computer Science & Engineering department has bagged various prestigious awards such as Largest CSI Student Branch, Young Researcher, and Best innovator. The department has MOUs with prestigious companies and institutes such as Cybersecurity Centre of excellence, TCS, Talent Sprint, Thalassemia and Sickle Cell Society, and many more. The girl students of the department enroll for “Women Empowerment Program” offered by Google, and “Amazon Girl Internship program” offered by Amazon and win best accolades. Every year TCS enrolls students from the department to train and certify them in the latest technologies and prepare them industry ready. We believe internships are complimentary to formal coursework, our students undergo paid national and international internships with companies as diverse as Google, Morgan Stanely, Amazon, JPMC, CHUBB, TCS, Cognizant and Zebra technologies, etc. Highest stipend of Rs. 110 K/month is offered by Google. The students from the department are recruited by leading IT companies as diverse as Amazon, Microsoft, Capgemini, TCS, JP Morgan, Deloitte, ServiceNow, etc. The highest pay package of 49 LPA has been offered by Google and an international pay package of 97 LPA is offered by Juniper Networks. The average pay package for our students is as high as 11 LPA. The first batch of CSBS students graduated this year (2023) are placed in Oracle, JPMC and TCS digital with pay packages of 18.5 LPA, 17.75 LPA and 7 LPA. Coming to higher education, the students have opted for masters in prestigious word class universities such as University of Texas, University of Florida, University of Illinois tec.
